Here are the names and categories for the winners of the 26th IOCCC:

    Edward Giles - WAV reverberator

    Joshua Karns - Breadth-first search on text
    US

    US

    FR

    Volker Diels-Grabsch - Self hashing
    DE
  • Most in need of transparency
    Don Yang - Text scrambler
    US
  • Most in need of debugging
    Yusuke Endoh - Backtrace quine
    JP
  • Most in need to be tweeted
    Christopher Mills - Machine Learning on text
    US
  • Best use of space and time
    Gil Dogon - X11 Golly game of Life
    IL
  • Most functional interpreter
    Ondřej Adamovský - Unlambda interpreter
    CZ
  • Most functional compiler
    Ben Lynn - Haskell compiler
